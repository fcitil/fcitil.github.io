Projects

NOVI

As a part of the ICE group, I have take a part in interdisciplinary project for novice drivers in METU Design Factory as a Part of DFGN. I not only worked on computer vision part of this project, but also other parts of the project since it was a interdisciplinary work.
Autonomous Dogfighter UAV

As an undergraduate project granted by AdımODTU, our team has designed and developed a dogfighting UAV system that is capable of identifying and tracking rival UAVs in the air. I was mainly responsible for the development of the airborne UAV tracking algorithm and deployment of the algorithms on an embedded onboard computer.
UORG 2022 Autonomous UAV Computer Vision Category

As a part of undergraduate group, I have developed image algorithms to detect multiple types of landmarks (characters, lines, geometric shapes) on the ground to feed a controller with error input for following a track.
Path Planning Algorithm for Swarm UAVs

As a part of a student group in METU IEEE RAS, we participated in national Swarm UAV competition. I mainly worked on path planning part of the project. I researched in general for path planning and implemented a viable path planning algorithm for our case.
UAV Gimbal Tracking System

Based on our knowledge in Autonomous Dogfighter UAV project, we developed a gimbal system consisting of UAV tracking and controller algorithm for 2 axis gimbal mechanism.
UAV Designs

As a part of a undergraduate student group, we participated in several UAV competitions. Apart from computer vision part of these projects, I was responsible of conceptual and mechanical design of the UAV using CAD programs. I also took a part in manufacturing processes of these projects in which I have experienced with composite materials and 3D printing.
